What is Organic Farming?

Organic farming is a method where crops are grown naturally without the use of chemical fertilizers and pesticides. It protects the soil, water, and environment, and produces healthy crops.

Benefits of Organic Farming:

  • Improves soil productivity
  • Provides healthy and nutritious food
  • Does not harm the environment
  • Reduces cost of farming

Popular Organic Techniques:

  • Using vermicompost as fertilizer
  • Jeevamrut and Beejamrut
  • Mulching and mixed cropping
