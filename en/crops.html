Information about Major Crops

Wheat

  • Sowing Time: October - November
  • Water Needs: Irrigation every 15 days
  • Fertilizer: Urea, DAP

Bajra (Pearl Millet)

  • Sowing Time: June - July
  • Water Needs: Low
  • Fertilizer: Organic manure preferred

Cotton

  • Sowing Time: May - June
  • Water Needs: Every 10-15 days
  • Pesticide: Use in controlled quantity

Vegetables

  • Different method for radish, okra, tomato etc.
  • Usually short-duration crops
  • Natural fertilizer is better
